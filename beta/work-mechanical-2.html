The Bolt

Power From Every Angle

I was involved with a group of Mechanical Engineering students designing an orientation-independent magnetic outlet adapter. This adapter would plug into a standard outlet and allow for rapid connection/disconnection with a specially designed power cord. This cord could then rotate 360 degrees while remaining attached and providing power, rendering this useful for cable management.

The Solution

I assisted with the circuit design, which consisted of concentric aluminum rings, with the ground ring set into the adapter making the adapter far more tamper-proof. The other two rings were for the hot wire and the neutral wire. Thick gauge wiring was used alongside highly conductive internal components, so that this product could be used on high voltage household circuits if desired.
