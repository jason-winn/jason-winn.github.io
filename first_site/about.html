As much as web design is a creative process, it is a key business tool. I understand this, and so over the years I've refined my process to help keep client goals in mind and to ensure all deadlines are met.

I strongly believe in keeping the client a part of the process as much as possible. I will listen to your ideas and help translate them to your website in the most seamless and affordable way possible. Read below to see specifically how the 'path' I follow can work for you.

Identify Your Online Goals


Sit down with me and discuss your goals for your site. We'll discuss the best way to both communicate your message and keep the experience enjoyable for the user.

Concept & Design Mockups


I will begin to draft site designs both by hand and in Photoshop. This is a great time to make big changes before the site goes into development.

Constructing the Site


This is where it all comes together and your site becomes functional. As construction of the site takes place, you will be given a testing server link at which you can view your site in its current state.

SEO, CMS, and More


Once you are satisfied with the site, I will add the finishing touches before it goes live. This includes optimizing the site for search engines, content systems, user tracking, and more.
